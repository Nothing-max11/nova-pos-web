Introduction

Welcome to the Nove POS documentation. This guide will help you set up and optimize your POS system for maximum efficiency.

What is Nove POS?

Nove POS is a comprehensive point of sale solution designed for retailers, restaurants, and service providers. It combines local-first reliability with cloud-sync power.

Installation

Setting up Nove POS is simple and fast. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Download the installer from our Download Page.
  2. Run the .exe file (Windows) or install the App (Android/iOS).
  3. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the setup.

Configuration

After installation, you need to configure your business settings:

  • Business Profile: Set your name, address, and tax ID.
  • Cloud Sync: Connect your Google Drive for automated backups.
  • Terminals: Add and name your POS terminals.

First Sale

Making your first sale is as easy as 1-2-3:

  1. Select items from your inventory or scan a barcode.
  2. Click the Checkout button.
  3. Select the payment method and complete the transaction.
