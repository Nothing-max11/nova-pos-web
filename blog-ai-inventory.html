Leveraging AI for Inventory & Spoilage Analytics

Data is the most valuable asset a retail or restaurant business generates. However, most small businesses lack dedicated data analysts to read their POS data, leading to hidden margin leaks through spoilage and inefficient pricing.

The Challenge of Margin Leakage

Tracking raw ingredients, identifying which items consistently expire before sale, and determining optimal markup pricing is incredibly tedious. While most POS systems provide raw Excel reports, business owners rarely have the time to synthesize this data into actionable business strategies.

The Nove POS AI Solution

The Nove POS integrated AI Assistant securely analyzes anonymized inventory data, detects spoilage trends, and suggests actionable pricing strategies directly within the POS dashboard.

Our built-in AI operates as a 24/7 business consultant, utilizing Large Language Models (LLMs) to perform complex analytics:

  • Privacy Shield: The system securely anonymizes business data. Users control exactly what information (Sales, Inventory, or Expenses) is shared with the AI engine.
  • Spoilage Detection: The AI cross-references sales velocity against inventory expiry dates, flagging items that are likely to spoil before they are sold and suggesting promotional discounts to clear the stock.
  • Conversational Queries: Instead of generating complex SQL queries, owners can simply type, *"What were my 3 least profitable items last week, and should I remove them from the menu?"* and receive a professional, data-backed answer instantly.
