Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Nove POS Enterprise.

How does the Hardware ID lock work?

Nove POS cryptographically binds your license to your computer's unique hardware signature. This ensures that your business data is only accessible on authorized devices, preventing unauthorized access even if your credentials are leaked.

Can I use Nove POS without internet?

Yes. Nove is built with a local-first architecture. All sales, inventory, and expense tracking work fully offline. Data will automatically sync to your secure cloud vault once a connection is restored.

Is my data shared with anyone?

Never. Your data is encrypted locally before it ever leaves your machine. We use enterprise-grade AES-256 encryption. Even our engineers cannot see your business sales or customer details.

Does the AI Assistant cost extra?

The Nove AI Engine is included in the Enterprise plan. However, you will need to provide your own Google Gemini API key (which has a generous free tier) to enable the intelligence features.
