Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know about Nova POS

How many computers can I use?

This depends on your plan. Basic allows 1 device, Premium allows 3, and Enterprise supports up to 25 connected computers in your network.

Is my data stored online or offline?

Nova POS uses a local-first architecture. All your business data is stored securely on your machine. In Premium and Enterprise plans, you can sync this data to your private Google Drive or our encrypted Firebase cloud for backup and remote access.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes! You can upgrade your license at any time. Simply contact us, and we will update your device license remotely without any data loss.

Do I need an active internet connection?

Not for sales. Nova POS works perfectly offline. Internet is only required for cloud synchronization, AI assistant queries, and license verification on startup.
